📋 Qué datos recolectamos
Requerido

Para que gastify funcione, guardamos los siguientes datos en servidores ubicados en la Unión Europea (residencia de datos AWS eu-west-1):

  • Email + nombre + país (registro)
  • Imágenes de boletas que escaneas (cifradas en reposo · eliminadas a los 90 días)
  • Ítems extraídos + categorías + montos + ubicaciones (duración de tu cuenta)
  • Telemetría anónima de uso (latencia, errores — no incluye contenido de tus boletas)
🔐 Mis derechos como titular
Informativo
Puedes ejercer en cualquier momento:
  • Derecho de acceso (ver qué datos tenemos sobre ti)
  • Derecho de rectificación (corregir datos incorrectos)
  • Derecho de supresión (eliminar tu cuenta y todos tus datos — "olvido")
  • Derecho de portabilidad (exportar todo en JSON o CSV)
  • Derecho de oposición (dejar de procesar para fines específicos)

Delegado de Protección de Datos: dpo@gastify.cl. Autoridad de reclamo: Agencia de Protección de Datos de Chile.

📊 Usos opcionales
Opcional
📋 Data we collect (GDPR art. 13)
Required

Under GDPR, we operate as Data Controller for your gastify account. Lawful basis: contract performance (art. 6.1.b).

  • Email, name, country (registration)
  • Receipt images (encrypted at rest · auto-deleted after 90 days)
  • Extracted items, categories, amounts, locations (account lifetime)
  • Anonymized usage telemetry (no receipt content)

Data residency: AWS eu-west-1 (Ireland). No data transferred outside EU without SCCs.

🔐 Your rights as data subject
Informational
GDPR rights (exercise anytime):
  • Right of access (art. 15) — see all data we hold on you
  • Right to rectification (art. 16) — correct inaccuracies
  • Right to erasure (art. 17) — "right to be forgotten"
  • Right to data portability (art. 20) — export in JSON/CSV
  • Right to object (art. 21) — stop specific processing
  • Right to restrict processing (art. 18)

DPO: dpo@gastify.eu. Supervisory authority: your national data-protection agency.

📊 Optional processing
Optional
📋 California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA)
Required

We collect the following categories of personal information (as defined by CCPA §1798.140):

  • Identifiers: email, name, IP address
  • Commercial information: receipts, purchase history
  • Geolocation: country, city (for currency + store matching)
  • Inferences: spending categories, merchant preferences

We do NOT sell your personal information. We do share aggregated anonymized data with our analytics provider (processor only, not controller).

🔐 Your rights (CA/CO/CT/VA/UT)
Informational
Consumer rights exercisable via privacy@gastify.com:
  • Right to know what PI is collected + sources + purposes
  • Right to delete (with exceptions per §1798.105)
  • Right to correct inaccurate PI
  • Right to opt-out of sale/sharing (we don't sell, but you can opt out preemptively)
  • Right to limit use of sensitive PI (none collected by gastify)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising rights
🚫 Do Not Sell / Do Not Share
Opt-out
📋 PIPEDA compliance
Required

Under PIPEDA (Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act), we collect only the personal information necessary to provide gastify services:

  • Identity (email, name)
  • Receipt images + extracted data
  • Usage telemetry (anonymized)

Data stored in AWS eu-west-1 (no Canadian data residency required under PIPEDA if consent given).

🔐 10 Fair Information Principles
Informational
Your rights under PIPEDA + Québec Law 25:
  • Access to your personal information (Principle 9)
  • Correction of inaccuracies
  • Withdrawal of consent at any time
  • Complaint to Privacy Commissioner of Canada
  • Québec residents: additional rights under Law 25 (automated decision-making, cross-border transfers)

Privacy contact: privacy@gastify.ca. Federal oversight: Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada.
