§4Erasure-vs-group policy — DECISIONS D82 rev 3

Two triggers — only leaving a group offers a choice

  1. Delete account (leaving the app) — total erasure, no choice. Delete own + the shared group copies → void the affected group-period stats (fact-based — can't show a stat once its data is gone) → notice "this member left the application; these stats were shut down for the affected months."
  2. Leave a group (keep the account) — the only choice: keep the already-shared copies (stats unchanged) or delete them (→ void affected stats + notice). The user's own data is untouched either way.
Mechanism: a tombstone (group, period, reason) the stats layer checks before display — voiding is O(mark), and a voided figure is stronger for privacy than a recomputed one (gone, not adjusted). Both deletion paths feed the same void; nothing is orphaned (account delete is total). ⚠ Engineering's read, not legal advice — DPO/counsel sign-off gated at Phase 5.
